Unfinished Business:

It is still a bit unclear if people will be available to meet during
the week. We need to address the ongoing issue of schedule incompatability
within the group. We need to come up with various solutions
that can provide an ultimate compromise so that we may succeed.

We also need to figure out if people are happy and content with their group roles/assignments.
We need to verify that people are in the role they desire to be in to maximize our labor efficiency.
Overall, it is imperative for everyone to take time out of their day to try and address these issues.

New Business:

The new issues that have come up have to deal with the design of the CSE110 project. Overall, it appears we have
NOT made any progress concerning any ideas about our web development goal that we want to achieve.
It is extremely concerning that a portion of the project is already around the corner, and
it appears we will not be able to submit something reasonable in time for the group presentations.

Another issue that has come up involves the lack of understanding of ones roles. Therefore, we need to come together to
discuss potential role changes within the group. We have also noticed that some groupmemebers have not been getting
enough rest. This appears to be an upcoming threat to our performance as a group, and needs to be addressed as soon
as possible.
